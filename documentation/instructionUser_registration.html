Registration

A self registration process is possible, if a new user knows the password of trial site.

For the registration three steps are necessary:

Account information

In the first step includes the choice of a username and a personal password. The username have to be a alphanumeric string, e.g. the email address. It is necessary to retype the password, to avoid a typo during the password choice.

To reach the next step of the registration process it is possible to click at the tab "Contact information" or at "Next".

Contact information

The following information are necessary:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email - for notification emails from the system
  • Phone number
  • Language - At the moment RANDI2 supports English and German


To reach the next step of the registration process it is possible to click at the tab "Trial site" or at "Next".

Trial site

In the last step it is necessary to select the trial site and to enter the corresponding password.

To complete the registration it is necessary to click the button "register".

After the successful registration the user can log in with the username and private password.
